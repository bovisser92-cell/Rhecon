About RheCon B.V.

A versatile and reliable company that combines management, construction, engineering and trading for a wide range of projects.

Who we are

RheCon B.V. — broad, technical, committed

RheCon B.V. is a Dutch company active at the intersection of management, construction, engineering and trading. We work for business clients who need a partner that thinks along, coordinates and also gets the work done.

Our broad orientation makes us deployable in a wide range of situations: as a project manager bringing structure, as a construction party that delivers, as an engineer who draws and prepares, or as a trading partner providing the right materials.

We are compact, direct and results-oriented. There are no unnecessary layers between you and the people doing the work.

Our core values

What we stand for

These values form the foundation of every collaboration and every project — regardless of scale or sector.

Quality

Our approach
