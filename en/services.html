Our services

RheCon B.V. is active in four disciplines: Management, Structures, Engineering and Trading. Below you can read what each of these pillars entails.

01 — Management

Organisation and direction for your project

RheCon B.V. provides management in the broadest sense. We act as project coordinator, directing party or organisational guide within technical and construction projects.

Good management is the backbone of every successful project. It prevents delays, miscommunication and cost overruns. We bring structure where it is needed and monitor progress so you can focus on your core business.

  • Project coordination and planning
  • Directing executing parties
  • Monitoring scope, budget and schedule
  • Reporting and progress monitoring
  • Organisational support in technical projects
02 — Structures

Building what matters

Under the heading of Structures, RheCon B.V. handles the actual realisation of construction projects. We are involved in the construction of complete industrial buildings, residential construction projects, building management and water engineering applications.

We work closely with clients, architects and contractors to deliver projects on time and within budget — with an eye for quality and durability.

  • Construction of complete industrial buildings
  • Residential construction projects
  • Building management
  • Water engineering projects
  • Realisation from foundation to final delivery
03 — Engineering

From concept to execution-ready drawings

RheCon B.V. provides technical engineering and work preparation for a wide range of construction and water engineering projects. We translate ideas or sketches into concrete, executable drawings and documents.

By keeping engineering in-house, the line between design and execution is short. Errors are identified early, communication is efficient and quality is assured.

  • Drawings and work preparation for industrial buildings
  • Drawings and work preparation for residential construction
  • Engineering for water engineering projects
  • Technical documentation and file formation
  • Coordination with contractors and executing parties
04 — Trading

The right materials, delivered directly

RheCon B.V. trades in steel, construction materials and contractor supplies. We deliver materials that meet project requirements — on time and at competitive terms.

Whether it's structural steel, construction materials for an industrial building or specific products for a water engineering project: we arrange the delivery so the contractor can build.

  • Trade in steel and steel construction products
  • Supply of construction materials for projects
  • Contractor materials and execution products
  • Direct delivery on site
  • Material advice based on project and specification requirements
